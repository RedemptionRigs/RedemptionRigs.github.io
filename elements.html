Project

 This was a project boat brought to me in the spring of 2025.
 I brought this project in for myself, so I had full creative freedom with the only constraint being this fishing boat had to be electric so we could fish quietly without disturbing the wildlife. The boat was barely used since its last registration in 1975, and the owner wanted to get it to someone who would appreciate it.
 After a few months of work, I was able to get it ready for the water again.



 After sanding, covering the whole bottom of the boat in epoxy, and paintning it with high quality marine paint, I was able to get the boat ready for the next step.
 I built a custom aluminum frame to hold the batteries and motor, and I was able to get the decking ready.
