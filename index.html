About Me

 What started as a bucket list project—refurbishing a worn-out jon boat I found online to turn it into a budget bass boat—quickly turned into something much more. I discovered how rewarding it was to bring an old boat back to life, and I realized there were others out there with boats full of potential, just waiting to be rebuilt.
 Now, I run a small custom jon boat refurbishing service focused on giving old boats a second chance. Whether you have a weathered hull collecting dust in your driveway or an idea for your dream fishing setup, I can help you bring it to life. Through my website, you can submit your boat, budget, and vision—and I’ll design a custom build that balances performance, practicality, and cost.
 At the heart of this project is a passion for craftsmanship, problem-solving, and making something meaningful out of the forgotten. I may still be a high school student, but this isn’t just a hobby—it’s a hands-on business I’m proud to grow with every build.
 Let's bring your boat back to life.

— Guido, Founder
